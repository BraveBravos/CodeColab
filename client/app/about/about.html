About Us

CodeColab was inspired by our previous experiences collaborating on projects and some of the common frustrations developers have. We wanted to maintain the workflow, but simplify the experience and combine many of the neccessary actions into one all inclusive coding hub.

What we promise

A way to commit, submit pull requests, and merge branches without your terminal or going to GitHub

No rebasing or merge conflicts

Automated branch creation to protect your code

Video chat for team communication

Ability to revert all changes by clicking arrow in the center of the diff

A way to deploy an application without going to Heroku
